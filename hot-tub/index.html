Before You Enter

A Better Soak Starts Here

Before You Hop In

  • Shower if possible.
  • Remove lotions and heavy oils.
  • Stay hydrated.
  • Never use glass containers in or around the spa.

During Your Soak

Enjoy Responsibly

While You're Relaxing

  • Children should always be supervised.
  • Keep the spa cover nearby while in use.
  • Enjoy responsibly if consuming alcohol.
  • If you begin to feel overheated, exit the spa and cool down.

When You're Finished

Leave It Ready for the Next Soak

Before Heading Inside

  • Turn off the jets if still running.
  • Replace the spa cover completely.
  • Secure the cover clips if practical.
  • Leave the water level unchanged.

Replacing the insulated cover helps keep the spa clean, warm, efficient, and ready for the next soak.

Health & Safety

Soak with Care

Hot tubs may not be appropriate for young children, pregnant guests, or anyone with certain medical conditions.

If you have health concerns, please consult your physician before using the spa.
