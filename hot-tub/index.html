Bigfoot's Basecamp

Hot Tub Guide

Relax, unwind, and enjoy one of the best parts of staying at Basecamp.

There's nothing quite like a soak under the mountain sky after skiing, hiking, or exploring Snoqualmie Pass. Our Sundance Spa is professionally maintained year-round so every guest can enjoy clean, relaxing water.

Spa Guide

Four Things to Know

Hours Safety

Quick Start

  • Hot tub hours are 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM.
  • Seats up to 7 guests.
  • No glass in or around the spa.
  • Kids must be supervised.
  • Shower if possible.

Hot tubs may not be appropriate for young children, pregnant guests, or anyone with certain medical conditions. If you have health concerns, please consult your physician before using the spa.

Relax Quiet Hours

While You Soak

  • Stay hydrated.
  • Enjoy responsibly if drinking.
  • Step out if you feel overheated.
  • Keep voices down during quiet hours.
  • Dry off before heading back inside.
Cover Water Care

When You're Done

  • Turn off the jets if still running.
  • Put the cover back on completely.
  • Secure clips if practical.
  • Leave the water level unchanged.
  • Keep food, lotions, and oils out of the water.
