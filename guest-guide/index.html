Hot Tub

Relax and Reset

Spa Hours

7:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Spa Information

Sundance Spas 800 Series

Model 800-OL (2022). Comfortably seats up to seven guests.

Please Help Us Keep It Ready

  • Shower before entering whenever possible.
  • Replace the cover after every use.
  • Secure the cover clips if practical.
  • No glass in or around the hot tub.
  • Keep children supervised at all times.

The Sundance spa is built to stay hot year-round. One favor before heading inside: pull the cover back over so it stays clean and warm for the next soak.

Before You Leave

A Simple Departure Checklist

  • Start the dishwasher if needed.
  • Place trash in the outdoor trash bin.
  • Return furniture to its original location if moved.
  • Lock all doors and windows.
  • Turn off lights.
  • Make sure the hot tub cover is securely replaced.
  • Travel safely!
