About Bigfoot's Basecamp

Bigfoot's Basecamp was created as a place where families and friends can slow down, reconnect, and enjoy the mountains without overthinking the plan.

Whether you're skiing fresh powder, hiking mountain trails, soaking under the stars, or hunting for hidden Bigfoots, we hope Basecamp becomes one of those places you're already talking about coming back to.
