About Bigfoot's Basecamp

Bigfoot's Basecamp was created as a place where families and friends can slow down, reconnect, and enjoy everything Snoqualmie Pass has to offer.

Whether you're skiing fresh powder, hiking mountain trails, soaking in the hot tub beneath the stars, or hunting for hidden Bigfoots, we hope your stay becomes part of your family's favorite traditions.
