Driving Tips

A Smoother Trip Up the Pass

Winter Roads

Winter Driving

  • Drive slower than normal.
  • Increase following distance.
  • Avoid sudden braking.
  • Keep emergency supplies in your vehicle.
Summer Roads

Summer Driving

  • Watch for wildlife.
  • Be aware of cyclists.
  • Expect weekend traffic.

Basecamp Tip

Our Advice

The weather can change dramatically within a single day in the Cascades. Even if the forecast looks perfect, bring an extra layer and check road conditions before leaving Basecamp. A few minutes of preparation can save a lot of mountain-day drama.
