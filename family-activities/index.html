Family Activities

Easy ideas for keeping the whole crew happy.

Whether you're here for a ski weekend, summer hiking, or a slower mountain getaway, Snoqualmie Pass has plenty for every age. These are some of our favorite family-friendly options near Basecamp.

Perfect for Little Kids

Easy Wins for All Ages

Easy Family Favorite Stroller Friendly

Gold Creek Pond

A beautiful paved trail looping around an alpine pond with big mountain views. Perfect for young kids, grandparents, and strollers.

Why We Love It

Probably the easiest way to get big mountain scenery with very little walking.

Scenic Waterfall All Ages

Snoqualmie Falls

One of Washington's most iconic waterfalls, with easy viewing platforms and a short walk.

Why We Love It

Kids love the huge waterfall, and adults don't exactly suffer through the views either.

Learn More Get Directions
Winter Family Favorite

Summit Tubing Center

One of our favorite winter activities for families. No skiing experience required—just grab a tube and enjoy nearly two hours of downhill fun with a covered conveyor lift back to the top.

Why We Love It

A great option for families that don't ski, or for skiers who want a low-effort snow day.

Packing Suggestions

Easy Family Checklist

Start early on summer weekends, check the weather, and bring the basics. Mountain plans go smoother when everyone has snacks.

  • Water bottles
  • Snacks
  • Sunscreen
  • Rain jacket
  • Extra layers for changing mountain weather
  • Comfortable shoes
  • Camera
  • Binoculars, optional
  • Give wildlife plenty of space
  • Leave No Trace so future families can enjoy these places too
